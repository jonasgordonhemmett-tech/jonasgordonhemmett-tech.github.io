Orange Duffel Bag

This is an orange bag I made for my mom. It features shoulder straps and a drawstring closure.

The pattern is from TonySeePatterns on Etsy and made some minor modifications like removing the handle.

My favorite part is the orange Clemence leather. I was able to get it for an amazing price as the piece I purchased came with some natural imperfections which I feel add character.
